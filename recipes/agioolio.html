Agio E Olio

Description

Agio e olio is a classic Italian pasta dish from Naples, Italy.

The name means "spaghetti with garlic and oil", highlighting its main ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water before draining.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring frequently, until the garlic is fragrant and just starting to turn golden, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to let the garlic burn.
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss well to coat the pasta evenly with the garlic-infused oil. If the pasta seems dry, add a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.
  4. Season the pasta with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Toss again to combine.
  5. Serve the aglio e olio pasta immediately, garnished with chopped fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
